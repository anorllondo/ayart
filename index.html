ABOUT THE ARTIST.

Artak Bagdasaryan

AЯRT (English: AYART, Russian: АЯРТ); real name Artak Bagdasaryan Valeryevich, (born August 26, 2005, Moscow, Russia) is a Russian performer and songwriter, co-founder of the DeEvreux label

More information

Artak was born in Moscow, but as an infant his family decided to move to Krasnodar, where he grew up and lived. He is Armenian by nationality He studied at school №65, played sports and was fond of hip-hop. While still at school he started writing his own lyrics and tracks inspired by modern Russian and Western sounds. He shared his budding creativity with his close friends, together with whom he formed Wanna Cry, which is when Artak's main path in music began. During his student years, while studying to be a programmer, Artak started playing guitar, further immersing himself in music, together with his old friend Bogdan Pakarev better known as DeEvreux, sound producer beatmaker and founder of the label of the same name. Artak finds himself in the genres of R&B, pop, hip-pop and the like, releasing his first official song “С тобой” (In English: "With You"). At the moment, the artist continues his university studies, but nevertheless gives all his priority to recording new tracks, developing his music career
